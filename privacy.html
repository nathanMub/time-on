Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026

At Time On Focus, we prioritize your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require user accounts. We do not collect personal identification information like names or email addresses unless you contact our support team directly.

2. Cookies and Advertisements

We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

3. Data Usage

Your timer settings and durations are stored locally in your browser and are not transmitted to our servers.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@timeonfocus.com.
